Our Story

A small bakery and kitchen on Main Street.

For years, Mason's Apron has been the place locals stop in for a coffee, where travellers break the journey on the N3, and where the cake counter has caused more than a few "I shouldn't, but I will" moments.

How we started

Built on the basics, done well.

Mason's Apron grew out of a simple idea: bake honest bread, cook the food you'd want yourself, treat people the way you'd want to be treated. No frills. No big claims.

Open since 2013, it's a small family-run team on Virginia's Main Street, with a counter full of fresh bakes by 9am, a kitchen turning out hot lunches by midday, and the same friendly faces serving regulars who've been calling in for years.

We supply locally too. Other cafés, shops and event organisers know they can ring us for a tray of scones or a custom cake and it'll be done properly.
