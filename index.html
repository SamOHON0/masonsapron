Family-run, friendly team

A bakery and kitchen that feels like home.

Mason's Apron has been part of life in Virginia for years. We're a small, family-run team putting genuine care into everything we serve, from a quick scone and coffee to a proper sit-down lunch.

People stop in on the way between Dublin and Cavan town, friends meet here for a catch-up, and locals come back week after week.
